**Project Overview**

OLA Ride Insights is a data analytics project developed to analyze ride-booking patterns, customer behavior, driver performance, cancellations, revenue trends, and operational efficiency using SQL, Python, and Power BI.
The project provides interactive visualizations and business insights that help stakeholders understand ride demand, revenue generation, customer preferences, and service quality.

**Objectives**
Analyze ride booking trends and customer behavior.
Monitor ride cancellations by customers and drivers.
Evaluate revenue generated across different payment methods.
Identify top-performing customers and drivers.
Analyze ride distance distribution and trip patterns.
Measure customer and driver ratings.
Create an interactive Power BI dashboard for business decision-making.
